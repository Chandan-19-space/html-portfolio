Chandan's Top Picks: The Best Kannada Movies You Must Watch

My Top Movies of All Time

Milana (2007):

Anjali is forced into marriage with Aakash by her father, but she can’t forget her past and asks for a divorce; they keep the divorce secret from his parents, and over time, unexpected events draw them closer.

Kousalya Supraja Rama (2023):

Rama, raised by a father who endorses toxic masculinity, loses his love due to his own ego; after his mother’s death he marries Muthulakshmi, helps her battle past trauma and addiction, confronts his flaws and ultimately changes for redemption and happiness.

Prithvi (2010):

Prithvi, an honest IAS officer posted in Bellary, exposes illegal mining causing pollution and takes on powerful mining lords aided by a journalist; though he faces threats and obstacles, he persists in his fight for justice.

Just Maath Maathalli (2010):

Siddharth, a singer who avoids romantic involvement after a tragic past, sets out to find Tanu and confess his love; along the way he narrates past misunderstandings including a fan’s suicide, grapples with guilt, and faces unexpected twists before the final resolution.
